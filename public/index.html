Project Capabilities

SOFTDOUGH is an all-in-one solution for donut shops, featuring:

Inventory Management

Efficiently manage your donut shop's inventory with real-time tracking and automated restocking alerts.

Production Orders

Streamline your donut production process with automated order management and scheduling.

Cost Calculation

Accurately calculate production costs, including ingredients and labor, to optimize pricing and profitability.

Income/Expense Tracking

Keep a detailed record of all financial transactions, providing insights into your business's financial health.

POS System

A user-friendly Point of Sale system tailored for donut shops, enabling quick and efficient customer transactions.
